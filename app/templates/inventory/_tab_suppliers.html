Vendors are the distributors, warehouse clubs, and online wholesalers you buy from. This is your address book + a sourcing workshop.
  • The yellow Open These Accounts banner tracks priority vendors that still need an account opened (research task #7.2). Click a status pill to cycle Not Started → Applied → Open.
  • Each card shows contact info, an origin chip (manual / imported / discovered / comparator), and a count of products you've sourced here. Click the count to filter the catalog.
  • Don't see who you need? Click Discover new vendors to run an AI search for wholesalers in your area.
  • Got a vendor's order guide? Open their card, click Edit, then paste the CSV or text into Import Prices to bulk-load offers.
  • To compare a single SKU across all vendors at once, use Find Product Prices.
