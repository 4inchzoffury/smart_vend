Email Queue — Review and approve AI-drafted replies to customer email.
  • The inbox is checked automatically throughout the day (no need to trigger it). Use Poll now to check on demand, or the Auto switch to change the interval or turn it off.
  • Each message is classified: genuine customer mail goes to Customer mail; vendor, promotional, internal, and unclassified mail goes to Other (filtered) with the reason it was sorted there. Nothing is deleted, and your Gmail inbox is never marked read.
  • Customer mail gets an AI draft (using your governance rules) ready to review. Edit it if needed, then Approve & Send to reply from your Gmail account.
  • Mis-sorted message? Use the person icon on a card to treat it as customer mail (this drafts a reply). Connect Gmail via the Connect Gmail Inbox button in the page header.
